Tyler Nicholas for SNYA

Representing École Française des Seychelles & The Youth of Tomorrow

A New Standard of Leadership

The youth of Seychelles do not just need a voice; we need structured, actionable representation. As a candidate for the Seychelles National Youth Assembly, my mission is to bring systemic precision, ethical governance, and strategic vision to the national stage. My work as a Membre Du Conseil de Vie Lycéenne at EFS has shown me the power of effective advocacy, and I am ready to scale that commitment to represent all young Seychellois.

My Core Platform

Digital Modernization

Leveraging my background in tech development and digital strategy to push for better IT infrastructure, digital literacy, and responsive e-governance for the youth.

Actionable Sustainability

Moving beyond talking points to real logistics. Advocating for structured community initiatives, much like the waste-reduction frameworks I oversee, to protect our islands.

Youth Entrepreneurship

Creating pathways for young Seychellois to build and scale their own ventures. I want to bring boardroom experience to the assembly to help draft policies that support youth-led startups.
