A Strategic Mandate for the Youth

The youth of Seychelles require more than just representation; we require a strategic mandate. As we navigate an increasingly complex global landscape, our national policies must reflect systemic precision, ethical governance, and forward-thinking diplomacy. Serving as a Membre Du Conseil de Vie Lycéenne at École Française des Seychelles has instilled in me a profound respect for institutional advocacy. Now, I seek to elevate that commitment to the national stage.

My candidacy for the Seychelles National Youth Assembly is built on the principle of actionable governance. I am not running to simply occupy a seat; I am running to ensure that votre voix is not only heard but translated into definitive policy. We must bridge the gap between our generation's potential and the legislative frameworks that govern votre avenir.

A Policy of Action

Gouvernance Numérique

Transitioning our civic engagement from outdated systems to transparent, youth-accessible digital frameworks. I will advocate for modernization within government communication channels to ensure absolute accountability to the public.

Développement Durable

Moving beyond diplomatic rhetoric to practical logistics. I will propose structured, assembly-backed initiatives focused on waste reduction and resource management to secure the ecological heritage of our archipelago.

Empowerment Économique

Drafting resolutions that lower the barrier to entry for young Seychellois entrepreneurs. By leveraging corporate insight, I aim to foster policies that support youth-led enterprises and technical skill development.

Faites Entendre Votre Voix

Effective diplomacy requires open channels. If you have a directive or a concern regarding our national youth policy, I invite you to open a dialogue.
