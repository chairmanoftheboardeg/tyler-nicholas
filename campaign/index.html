A Strategic Mandate for the Youth

The youth of Seychelles require more than just representation; we require a strategic mandate. My candidacy for the Seychelles National Youth Assembly is built on the principle of actionable governance. I am not running to simply occupy a seat; I am running to ensure that votre voix is translated into definitive, structured policy.

A Policy of Action: The Core Directives

Youth Empowerment in Schools & Communities

Objective: To strengthen youth leadership, responsibility, and civic engagement.

  • School Leadership Framework: Establish structured Youth Representative Councils and monthly dialogue sessions with school administration.
  • Community Action Projects: Provide small government-backed micro-grants for youth-led community initiatives.
"Youth should not be spectators of development. They should be architects of it."

National Youth Work Experience Scheme (NYWES)

Objective: A structured, accessible pathway for students to gain work experience during holidays and weekends.

  • Centralised Portal: A unified online platform listing holiday internships and volunteer opportunities for secondary students.
  • Employer Incentives: Recognition certificates and future tax incentives for businesses hosting students.
  • Structured Outcomes: Minimum learning requirements and official certificates of completion issued nationally.

Digital Skills & Future Careers

Objective: Preparing Seychelles youth for global competition beyond traditional sectors.

  • Free coding and digital literacy workshops.
  • AI and technology awareness seminars.
  • Partnerships with local ICT companies for certification opportunities.
"Not every youth will work in tourism. The digital economy has no island borders."

Youth Skills Development Workshops

Objective: Equipping students with the practical tools needed to enter the professional world confidently.

  • Nationally organized CV writing sessions and Interview Training.
  • Public speaking and corporate communication workshops.
  • Entrepreneurship basics for young innovators.

National Inter-School Exchange Program

Objective: Fostering national unity and shared educational best practices among Seychellois institutions.

  • Ambassadorial Exchange: A structured 1-week program where students attend a different institution across the islands.
  • Knowledge Transfer: Students observe different school cultures, student council operations, and academic environments while maintaining their coursework.
  • Implementation: Returning students act as ambassadors, bringing back constructive ideas and operational improvements to implement in their home schools.

Faites Entendre Votre Voix
