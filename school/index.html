My Academic Foundation

The Academic Rigor

My education at Ecole Française des Seychelles is the technical backbone of my future. By specializing in Mathematics and Physics-Chemistry, I am actively engineering the precise skill set required for advanced aerospace studies and commercial flight training. The French educational system demands a high level of analytical thinking, which translates perfectly into my daily operational planning.

Student Leadership

Beyond the core sciences, my time at EFS involves active participation in student governance. Serving as a Membre Du Conseil de Vie Lycéenne allows me to advocate for student initiatives, bridge the gap between administration and the student body, and practice the diplomatic leadership skills I use daily as Chairman of TNG.

Balancing Act

Combining a demanding high school schedule with deep interests in Politics, Entrepreneurship, and Virtual Airline Management requires immense discipline. EFS provides the challenging environment necessary to test my limits and prepare me for the high-pressure environments of both the business world and the aviation industry.
